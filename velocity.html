Business Law Firm Overview


Photo credit: Marcelo Marcos Barquera 2016

Nancy Fallon-Houle owns and operates a boutique Business Law, Corporate Law, & Securities Law firm formed in 1998, as "Nancy Fallon-Houle, P.C." with a name change in 2014 to "Velocity Law, LLC". Located in west-suburban Chicago, Downers Grove, in DuPage County. Nancy represents clients who are entrepreneurs, small businesses, mid-sized businesses, private investors, and people who conduct business in the securities & futures trading communities. Nancy is a business attorney with over 25 years of experience in incorporations, transactional law, corporate law, and securities law. Her small law firm of 2 to 3 people, represents clients in downtown Chicago, suburban Chicago, and sprinkled throughout the US.

Nancy's mission is to provide practical business law advice and excellent quality legal documents, at a reasonable price, with personal service and uncompromising ethics. She capitalizes on the efficiency of technology, using it to the hilt in the delivery of legal services. One of her highest priorities is to produce documents written in clear, concise, plain English - understandable by clients, their business partners, and their investors.

Nancy trained in excellence, accuracy and deal completion from a large, downtown Chicago law firm, Katten Muchin Zavis, and implements those qualities into her practice in a small law firm setting, while focusing on the needs of business clients.

As a business lawyer, Nancy handles business law matters including: Incorporation, LLC formation, business formation and counsel, business transactions and agreements, buying a business, selling a business, business relationships, adding business partners, business split-ups, business dissolutions, redemption or removal of shareholders, shareholder agreements, LLC Agreements, private placements and private offerings, Reg D 506 and Blue Sky, private equity investments, venture capital investing, private companies issuing stock to employees or investors, and acting as issuer's counsel in Regulation D Rule 506(b) and 506(c) private offerings and venture capital transactions.

Law Firm Location: 5449 Bending Oaks Place, Downers Grove, Illinois, DuPage County, in Chicago's west suburbs, near the intersection of I-88 and I-355.
